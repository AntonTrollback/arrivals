FAQ

Does the price include taxes and shipment?

Yes. Each work prized on ARRIVALS includes local taxes and safe delivery


How long time will it take before I get my piece?

The delivery time depends on where you live. Expect about two to three week delivery unless otherwise specified. Often your order will arrive faster.


Is the artwork available in variety of sizes?

The work on ARRIVALS is only available in one size unless other is remarked.


Is it possible to see the artwork "live" before buying it online?

Yes. You are more than welcome to drop by our headquarters, NAU gallery, at Hudiksvallsgatan 4b in Stockholm. There you can see an example of all the work sold on ARRIVALS, and even buy it right away! NAU gallery is open Wed-Fri, 12.00 - 18.00 and Sat-Sun, 12.00-16.00


How is the piece packaged?

All pieces sold on ARRIVALS are delivered safely, packed in silk paper and a sturdy cardboard tube.


What happens if I am not happy with my purchase?

ARRIVALS offers a 14 days full return on pieces as long as the piece is still in mint condition. This does however not apply for photographs, which are print-on-demand.


What does "signed and limited edition" mean?

This means that the work is signed by the artist, and printed only in a limited number of copies. When the edition is sold out it won’t be available for purchase again.


If a piece is "sold out" what does it mean?

If it says that piece is sold out it means that the total limited edition is sold out and that it won’t be available for purchase again.
